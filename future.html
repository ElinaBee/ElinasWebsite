Aspirations

If the world would be perfect and I would be able to do something I have been dreaming for a while now then it definitely would be Theatre/Set design. I have transferable skills that can can be used in this job and I just love that you need to create a little world for the actors and spectators. But for now I want to learn new things and continue to grow as a person.
A set designer is in charge of designing and creating the sets that appear in films and television programmes as well as in the theatre. The role involves working with and communicating with directors, producers, costume designers and other members of staff.
